CATAMI

Measuring our dinner

Welcome to the Collaborative and Annotation Tools for Analysis of Marine Imagery and Video (CATAMI) project. CATAMI is a collection of federal funded eResearch projects by the Australian National Data Service (ANDS) and the National eResearch Collaboration Tools and Resources (NeCTAR) designed to assist marine ecology and habitat monitoring processes.
